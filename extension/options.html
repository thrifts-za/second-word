Only versions available to this app through YouVersion appear here. Your choice stays in this browser.

What this sees

  • Only the active text box you are writing in. With “Notice on its own” off, nothing leaves your browser until you press Second Word.
  • On Gmail, automatic reflection can include the latest message you are replying to—not quoted history, addresses, recipients, or the rest of the thread.
  • Nothing is stored. There is no account and no history.

Gmail has reply-aware context. On other supported text boxes, Second Word works from the draft you choose to reflect on.
